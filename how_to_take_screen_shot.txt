
How to take a screen shot of Golly
----------------------------------

Start Golly:
- click on a python program "???.py" that generates an image in Golly
- it's up to you to write that python program

Screen Shot: 
- using Keyboard, press Windows logo key (four squares) + Shift + S
- press the three keys in that order, holding all three keys down

Save Photo:
- now we're clicking the mouse, instead of pressing keys
- click on Windows logo (four squares) at bottom of screen
- click on Photos
- your screen shot should be visible
- double click on the desired image
- click on three dots "..." at top of screen
- click on "Save as"
